Research

The search for materials based on density functional theory (DFT) combined with machine learning (ML) involves areas such as the design of various metal-sulfur battery cathode materials (sodium-sulfur batteries, lithium-sulfur batteries, aluminum-sulfur batteries), the design of diverse electrocatalyst materials (HER, OER, ORR), and the design of gas-sensitive materials.

The master's thesis was titled "Study on TM3 (HXTP)2 Metal-Organic Frameworks as Gas-Sensing Materials Based on Density Functional Theory Calculations and Machine Learning," and the undergraduate thesis was titled "Design of a 5 kW Standalone Solar Power System for Rural Households."

The upcoming research will focus on machine learning-assisted high-throughput screening for gas adsorption and separation MOF materials.

Proficient with VASP, Python/Scikit-learn, Origin, Materials Studio, VESTA, RASPA, and experienced in assisting with server management.
